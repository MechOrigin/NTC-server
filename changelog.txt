Added combiner recipes (mekanism) for making aeternalis fuel. Blocks and item types supported.
It's a 1:4 ratio: ex. 4 blocks of Aeternalis fuel get 1 block of Dark alchemical coal. Same with item types.

Added blocks for Auxillium fuel types alchemical coal, mobius fuel and their subsequent crafting recipes.

Changed RFtools crafter from 10 operations in fast mode to 5.

There are all mostly Performance improvements because crafting in AE2 and RFtools crafter consumes a lot of server TPS.
